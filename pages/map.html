The Map of Helion

The map of Helion is vast and varied, with many different regions and terrains to explore.

The center of the map is dominated by a large, sprawling forest, home to many mythical creatures such as elves, fairies, and dryads. The forest is bordered by mountains to the north and east, where dwarves and dragons reside. To the west is a vast ocean, where sailors brave the treacherous waters to reach distant lands. In the east, there are deserts and arid plains where nomadic tribes wander, while beyond them lie vast, uncharted territories shrouded in mystery and danger.

The map is filled with kingdoms and empires, each with its own unique culture, architecture, and history. Overall, the map is a rich and fascinating depiction of a world full of magic and wonder, waiting to be explored.

The World, other continents and other dimensions

Helion is only one continent in the world of Gaia. Outside of the map there are other continents with other timelines and races. Furthermore, Hell is another plane of existence outside Helion where the demons live. But there are many other dimensions. For instance, Hell was truly made to be a counterpart to Heaven. A dimension where the Angels and the gods live.

Another dimension is the vigil, the world of dreams. Then there is the void, the dimension of nothingness. The beyond, an unknown dimension where Lovecraftian horrors inhabit it. And so on and so forth. Helion is just a one fraction of a huge world.
