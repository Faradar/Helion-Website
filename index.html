Welcome to the Continent of Helion

What is Helion?

Helion is the name of a medieval fantasy setting set in the world of Gaia, in the continent of Helion.

In this setting, you can find creatures such as elves, humans, dragons, and many others who live together in a delicate balance. The alignments of good and evil are at the core of this setting, for creatures can take great power by exercising great good or great evil.

With danger lurking around every corner and wonders to be discovered, this world promises endless possibilities for those brave enough to venture forth.

The Races

There are many races in this setting, each with their own strengths and weaknesses. Some beholden to good, others to evil, and others who are free to choose, or not able to at all.

The Characters

There are millions of beings that walk the vast continent of Helion. Some, however, have achieved feats so great or so terrible that they have etched their name in history. Here we will talk about some of the most interesting ones.

And much more!

The alignments, the artifacts, the creatures, the beasts of Helion, there is just so much to talk about. But don't feel overwhelmed. Take it one step at a time. If you like the setting, feel free to use it for a campaign using whatever TTRPG (tabletop role-playing game) system you feel most comfortable with.

Trending Character

Kaiden is a legendary blind warrior who has honed his other senses, such as hearing and touch, to a level that rivals those with sight. His story is one of determination and resilience, proving that even in a world of magic and mythical creatures, a blind human can still become a hero.

Trending Destination

Sylvania is the capital city of the elves. It's a marvel of beauty and magic, located deep within the forest, its air is filled with the sweet fragrance of flowers and herbs. The city is a shining example of their dedication to living in harmony with nature.
